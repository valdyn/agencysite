ABOUT

We are a studio located in Paris.

Founded by Enrik Johansen in 2003 ARD DESIGN collaborate across disciplines and industries to bring buildings and infrastructure projects to life.Our design practice works with public and private organizations to create inspiring facilities.

DESIGN

We seek a balance between art and technology, form and function, beauty and purpose,vision and result.We serving clients by listening to their concerns, understanding their goals, and sharing in their vision.
